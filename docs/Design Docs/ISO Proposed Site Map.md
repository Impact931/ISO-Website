## **2\. Homepage Wireframe (The Interactive Gateway)**

| Section | Interactive Module | User Experience Goal |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |

| **2\. PROBLEM (Interaction)** | **"The Focus Slider":** A draggable slider over an image. Left: "The moment you missed" (Blurry). Right: "The ISO Memory" (Perfect). **Caption:** "Stop gambling with your memories." | **Visceral Proof:** Physically fixing the problem. |
| **3\. GUIDE (Chat)** | **"ISO AI Guide" Bubble:** Pops up: "Hi\! I can help you find your photos or book a team shoot." | **Immediate Assistance.** |
| **4\. PLAN (Scrollytelling)** | **"The Journey Timeline":** A vertical SVG line draws itself down the center as you scroll. **Stop 1:** Register (Icon animates) **Stop 2:** Game Day (Camera shutter sound/animation) **Stop 3:** Delivery (Phone notification animation). | **Guided Tour:** "Walking the path." |
| **5\. SUCCESS (Carousel)** | **"Wall of Fame":** Horizontal swipeable gallery of "Hero Moments." | **Social Proof.** |

## **3\. ICP Sub-Page Wireframes (The Guided Tours)**

### **A. /parents (Sheree's Journey)**

* **Hero:** Video loop of a mom relaxing in the stands while ISO shoots.  
* **Interactive Module:** **"The Memory Vault"** \- A scrollable comparison of "Digital Files" vs. "Heirloom Prints" (Pillar 4).  
* **Trust Interaction:** Hover over a "Safety Badge" to see the checklist popup (Background Check, SafeSport, Insurance).  
* **FAQ:** Accordion style.

### **B. /organizations (Cathy's Journey)**

* **Hero:** Split screen: "Chaos" (Papers flying) vs. "ISO Control" (Calm tablet dashboard).  
* **Interactive Tool:** **"Profit Simulator"**  
  \[Slider: 500 Athletes\] \-\> \[Slider: $10 Rebate\] \-\> **Result: $5,000 Raised.**  
* **The Roadmap:** Animated timeline showing "We handle Scheduling \-\> Shooting \-\> Support."

### **C. /athletes (Jordan's Journey)**

* **Hero:** High-energy "Hype Reel" (Fast cuts, music).  
* **Interactive Module:** **"Style Selector"**  
  Click buttons: \[Smoke\] \[Gel Lights\] \[Clean Studio\]. Image updates instantly to show the look.  
* **Social Proof:** Ticker tape of Instagram handles/comments.

### **D. /photographers (Frank's Journey)**

* **Hero:** POV shot looking through a viewfinder.  
* **Interactive Tool:** **"Earnings Calculator"**  
  \[Slider: 4 Gamedays\] x \[Slider: $300 Rate\] \= **Monthly Side Hustle Income.**  
* **CTA:** "Apply to Network."
